Online:

Sign in or register for My Account or the MyBenefits CRA mobile application so you can get your personalized benefit information.

What you can do online:

  • apply for child benefits
  • view Canada Child Benefit and related provincial and territorial programs payments, account balance, and statement of account
  • view and update children in your care
