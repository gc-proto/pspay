Canada child benefit

Claiming the benefit

  1. Before you apply
  2. How to apply
  3. After you apply
  4. How we calculate your benefit

  6. Contact us

The Canada child benefit (CCB) is a tax-free monthly payment made to eligible families to help them with the cost of raising children under 18 years of age.

The CCB might include the child disability benefit and any related provincial and territorial programs.

To get the CCB, you have to file your return every year, even if you did not have income in the year. If you have a spouse or common-law partner, they also have to file a return every year.
