What you'll need

To verify your identity and to set up the direct deposit, you'll need

  • social insurance number
  • current address, including postal code
  • most recent income tax and benefit return and information about the most recent payments you got from the Canada Revenue Agency
  • three-digit bank (financial institution) number, five-digit transit number, and your account number

In person

Some banks and financial institutions in Canada will be able to assist you in completing a direct deposit enrolment for one or several of your Government of Canada payments. This service is currently offered by:

  • Desjardins
  • TD Canada Trust

By mail
  1. Complete the Canada Direct Deposit enrolment form
  2. Mail the completed form to the address on the form

Note that it might take up to 3 months to process the mailed form

Trusts

Set up or update direct deposit and bank details for your trust

CRA payments that you can sign up to receive by direct deposit

Sign up for direct deposit as a trust if you are the trustee, custodian, executor, or other type of legal representative for a trust receiving a payment.

By mail

  1. Complete the Direct deposit request for T3 (T3-DD)
  2. Mail the completed form to the address on the form

Alternative: You can complete the direct deposit area of the applicable T3 Trust Income Tax and Information Return (T3RET)
