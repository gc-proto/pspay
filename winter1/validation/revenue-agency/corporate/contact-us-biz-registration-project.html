Online self service

Select the service you want to register for or log in to:

My Account for Individuals
Allows individuals to track their refund, view or change their return, check their RRSP limit, etc.

My Business Account
Allows businesses to access their GST/HST, payroll, corporation income taxes, excise taxes, excise duties, etc.

Represent a Client
Access tax information on behalf of individuals and businesses, including your employer.
