What to do if you're having pay issues

Answer some questions to get personalized guidance for your issue.

What kind of issue are you having?

Did you start or return to work within the last 6 weeks?

If all the paperwork was submitted on time, your first pay should be deposited within your first 20 days of work.

  • If you have not received any pay within your first 20 days of work, you can request an Emergency Salary Advance.
  • Check the Pay Calendar to understand the timing of pay periods with paydays and your first pay.

First thing to do: Talk to your manager

Your first step is to report that you weren't paid to your manager or someone in your management chain.

  • Your manager can immediately request an Emergency Salary Advance.
  • If no one in your management chain is available to talk to you right away, you should contact the Pay Help Centre
