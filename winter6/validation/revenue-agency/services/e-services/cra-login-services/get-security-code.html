  • My Business Account
  • Represent a Client

Use your online banking credentials

You should be able to register with your online bank credentials using the "Sign-in partners login" for any CRA account. You should not need a security code.

  • Sign in to My Account
  • Sign in to My Business Account
  • Sign in to Represent a client
  • Sign in to a mobile app, non-resident tax representative or candidate profile

Step 1. Register for My Account (personal) or My Business Account

To register My Account and the other accounts listed below, you will need the following:

Register for My Account
Sign in with limited access

You can get limited service after completing step 1, similar to a Guest account by beginning the registration process and proving your identity. You do not need to finish the full registration process (mail or call for a security code, set up security questions etc.) to access the following:

  • View status of your tax return
  • Notification preferences
  • Request a remittance voucher
  • View notice of assessment or reassessment (simple view, lines X, Y and Z)
  • View RRSP deduction limit and TFSA contribution room
  • Pay by pre-authorized debit (create new agreement)

Try My Account as a guest

Step 2. Get a CRA security code for full access to My Account

To complete your registration for My Account, you will need to get a security code. You can do this by phone or by mail.
