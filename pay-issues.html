What to do if you're having pay issues

Steps to take to address the issue.

  1. Inform your manager

    You have to tell your manager or supervisor first. If you had no pay, your manager may be able to take immediate action like issuing an emergency salary advance.

  2. Report the issue

    Departments are either served by the Pay Centre or by their own departmental compensation advisors. Find out by choosing your department in this menu:

    If your department is served by the Pay Centre, then you must contact them. You can do that by filling out the Phoenix feedback form or by calling.

    • Phoenix feedback form
    • Call 1-855-343-3434 or outside North America 1-613-343-3434

    If your department isn't served by the Pay Centre, you should contact a departmental compensation advisor.

  3. There are also cases where your manager needs to submit a pay action request form, but it's hard to tell that now.
