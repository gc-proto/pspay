Paying on time

We consider you to have made your tax payment on the day that:

  • it is received by the CRA
  • it is processed at any financial institution belonging to the Canadian Payments Association

Payments made at an Automated Teller Machine (ATM) may not be processed that same day.

If your payment due date falls on a Saturday, a Sunday, or a public holiday, the payment will be considered received on time if it is received on the first business day after the due date.

If you can't pay in full

If you are not able to make your next payment to the CRA in full, see:

  • Make a payment arrangement to pay your debt over several months
