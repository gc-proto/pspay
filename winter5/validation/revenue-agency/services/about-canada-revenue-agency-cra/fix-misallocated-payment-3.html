Already late

If the due date has already passed:

  • you may face an interest rate compounded daily at the prescribed rate
  • you may face more serious financial or legal consequences

If you want to know more about penalties for late payments:

  • If you refuse to pay and cooperate with the Canada Revenue Agency

What you can do

If the payment has not been made because of negligence:

  • make your payment immediately to avoid further interest charges

If you have circumstances that prevent you from making the full payment:

  • contact the CRA as soon as possible
  • let us know about your situation and help you create a payment arrangement that addresses your circumstances and to minimize the penalties associated with a late payment

Late on a payment arrangement payment

Be careful not to miss a payment that you have already made a payment arranagement for, or have any unfiled tax returns while in a payment arrangement. We may cancel the payment arrangement and take action to recover your debt.

If you still can't make your payment after a payment arrangement has been made, contact us immediately to discuss a solution.
